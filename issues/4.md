**Objective**  
Summarize and critically evaluate prior research papers and documented results to identify the most relevant findings that align with our goals for automated chest X-ray diagnosis using CNNs and LLMs.

**Tasks**  
- Review previously published research papers and documented experimental results.  
- Extract and summarize key methodologies, findings, and conclusions.  
- Assess the relevance and impact of each source in the context of our current research direction.  
- Justify the selection of findings based on criteria such as methodological rigor, novelty, and applicability.  
- Organize selected findings and supporting rationale in a clear format for team-wide review.

**Outcome**  
A curated selection of impactful and relevant findings from prior research, chosen to guide model design, benchmarking, and future experimentation.
